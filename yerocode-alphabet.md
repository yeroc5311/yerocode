# yerocode
remaking the alphabet to make it look like spammed textâ„¢

chars used:
```
~ ! @ # $ % ^ & * - + = | \\\ /
```

---

- every english letter maps to exactly one symbol or symbol-pair
- same length as normal text (mostly)
- readable once you memorize like 10 rules

---

|-------|----------|
